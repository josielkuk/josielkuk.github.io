Research

I do research on Evolutionary Computation. My recent work include:
  • Evolutionary Algorithms (mainly MOEA/D, NSGA-III and some variants)
  • Hyper-Heuristics
  • Adaptive Operator Selection (Multi-Armed Bandit, Adaptive Pursuit, Probability Matching, Thompson Sampling)
  • Fitness Landscape Analysis
  • Multi and Many-objective Optimization
