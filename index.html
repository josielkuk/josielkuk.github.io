Research

I do research on Evolutionary Computation. My recent work include Evolutionary Algorithms (mainly MOEA/D, NSGA-III and some variants), Hyper-Heuristics, Adaptive Operator Selection (Multi-Armed Bandit, Adaptive Pursuit, Probability Matching), Multi-Objective Optimization, and others.
